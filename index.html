About us

As a shop, we've been around for over 40 years. As one of the longest standing shops in the Dublin area, we know a thing or two about bikes.
Our mechanics have a collective 140 years of experience behind them, catering to all styles of bicycles and cyclists.

  • Specialised in racing/road bikes.
  • Carbon frame repairs.
  • carbon wheel building specialists.
  • Road cycling club with 200+ members established in 1985.
